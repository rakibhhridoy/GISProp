\section*{Business Opportunity}

Precinct Smart is building a \textbf{geospatial intelligence platform} that brings together LiDAR, photogrammetry, and data science to serve Bangladesh's growing demand for advanced mapping and agricultural technology. No other provider in Bangladesh currently combines all three: \textcolor{AccentGreen}{\textbf{LiDAR + Data Science + NGO distribution}}.

\section*{The Market}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Bangladesh LiDAR Market:} USD 17-34M (growing 20\% annually)
    \item \textbf{Farmer Adoption Proof:} 127,500+ farmers already using drones (World Bank 2023), seeing 15-60\% yield improvements
    \item \textbf{Addressable Market:} USD 500K-1.5M opportunity in first 3 years
    \item \textbf{Growth Drivers:} Infrastructure mega-projects (Padma Bridge, Dhaka Metro), agricultural modernization, government digitalization
\end{itemize}

\section*{Business Model}

\textbf{Four Revenue Streams:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Data Collection:} BDT 50K-500K per mission (35\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Processed Maps:} BDT 200K-1.5M per project (50\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Farmer Apps:} BDT 5K-20K/farmer/year via NGO partnerships (65-85\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Consulting:} BDT 2M-5M per engagement (70\% margin)
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{What competitors can't easily copy:} NGO partnerships with BRAC and ASA open a channel to 10M+ farmers. Partners earn a 15--20\% commission --- a meaningful new income stream for them --- while Precinct Smart gains farmer distribution that would take years to build independently.

\section*{Regulatory Path}

CAAB (Civil Aviation Authority Bangladesh) requirements are well-defined, and the path to compliance is straightforward:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Pilot certification: 4-6 weeks via Avion Aerospace Academy (BDT 8-12L)
    \item Drone registration: 2-3 weeks (BDT 10-20K per aircraft)
    \item Flight approvals: 45-day FSR requirement (planned campaigns)
    \item Insurance: BDT 50-100L liability coverage (BDT 3-5L annual)
    \item \textbf{Timeline to first operations:} 6-8 weeks
\end{itemize}

\section*{Implementation}

\textbf{Year 1 (Foundation):} Hire the team, get certified, win 5--8 government and commercial projects, hit break-even by Month 10.

\textbf{Year 2 (Growth):} Launch the farmer app platform through NGO partners (5K farmers), open 2 regional hubs, reach BDT 96.5L revenue at a 51\% margin.

\textbf{Year 3 (Scaling):} Scale to 16K farmers, operate 4 hubs, and convert the platform into a high-margin engine --- BDT 204L revenue, 67\% margin.

\section*{Farmer Applications (ML-Powered)}

Three applications, each with a clear and measurable return for the farmer:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Yield Prediction:} 5-15\% yield improvement = BDT 50-200K farmer value
    \item \textbf{Disease Detection:} Prevent 10\% crop loss = BDT 100-300K farmer value
    \item \textbf{Water Planning:} 30\% water savings = BDT 30-80K farmer value
\end{itemize}

At BDT 5--8K per year, the payback period for a farmer is 3--12 weeks. Those are adoption economics that work.
