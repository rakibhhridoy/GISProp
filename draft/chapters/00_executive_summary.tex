\section*{Business Opportunity}

Precinct Smart is establishing a \textbf{geospatial intelligence platform} combining LiDAR, photogrammetry, and data science to serve Bangladesh's rapidly growing demand for advanced mapping and agricultural technology. Precinct Smart is positioned to be the \textcolor{AccentGreen}{\textbf{only provider combining LiDAR + Data Science + NGO distribution}} in Bangladesh.

\section*{The Market}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Bangladesh LiDAR Market:} USD 17-34M (growing 20\% annually)
    \item \textbf{Farmer Adoption Proof:} 127,500+ farmers already using drones (World Bank 2023), seeing 15-60\% yield improvements
    \item \textbf{Addressable Market:} USD 500K-1.5M opportunity in first 3 years
    \item \textbf{Growth Drivers:} Infrastructure mega-projects (Padma Bridge, Dhaka Metro), agricultural modernization, government digitalization
\end{itemize}

\section*{Business Model}

\textbf{Four Revenue Streams:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Data Collection:} BDT 50K-500K per mission (35\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Processed Maps:} BDT 200K-1.5M per project (50\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Farmer Apps:} BDT 5K-20K/farmer/year via NGO partnerships (65-85\% margin)
    \item \textbf{Consulting:} BDT 2M-5M per engagement (70\% margin)
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Key Differentiator:} NGO partnerships with BRAC and ASA reach 10M+ farmers. NGO partners receive a 15--20\% commission, creating a new revenue stream for them while Precinct Smart gains distribution access to farmers that competitors cannot replicate.

\section*{Regulatory Path}

CAAB (Civil Aviation Authority Bangladesh) requirements are clear and achievable:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Pilot certification: 4-6 weeks via Avion Aerospace Academy (BDT 8-12L)
    \item Drone registration: 2-3 weeks (BDT 10-20K per aircraft)
    \item Flight approvals: 45-day FSR requirement (planned campaigns)
    \item Insurance: BDT 50-100L liability coverage (BDT 3-5L annual)
    \item \textbf{Timeline to first operations:} 6-8 weeks
\end{itemize}

\section*{Implementation}

\textbf{Year 1 (Foundation):} Build team, get certified, land 5-8 government/commercial projects, reach break-even

\textbf{Year 2 (Growth):} Launch farmer apps with NGO partners (5K farmers), add services, open 2 regional hubs, reach BDT 96.5L revenue (51\% margin)

\textbf{Year 3 (Scaling):} Scale to 16K farmers, open 4 hubs, full profit realization, BDT 204L revenue (67\% margin)

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Yield Prediction:} 5-15\% yield improvement = BDT 50-200K farmer value
    \item \textbf{Disease Detection:} Prevent 10\% crop loss = BDT 100-300K farmer value
    \item \textbf{Water Planning:} 30\% water savings = BDT 30-80K farmer value
\end{itemize}

Farmer price: BDT 5-8K/year (3-12 week payback period) --- compelling economics for adoption.
