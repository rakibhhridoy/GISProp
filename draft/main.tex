\chapter*{Executive Summary}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Executive Summary}

Precinct Smart is building a premium geospatial services company in Bangladesh, combining drone-mounted LiDAR and photogrammetry with applied data science. The business spans four sectors --- agriculture, government, real estate, and the environment --- offering everything from raw data collection and processed maps to custom software applications and strategic consulting.

\vspace{1cm}

\textcolor{SecondaryBlue}{\textbf{Key Business Parameters:}}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Business Model:} Startup (newly formed)
    \item \textbf{Initial Investment:} BDT 100+ lakhs (USD 120,000+)
    \item \textbf{Team Structure:} Core in-house + External consultants
    \item \textbf{Growth Target (Year 1-3):} 5 → 20 → 50+ projects
    \item \textbf{Competitive Edge:} Advanced LiDAR + ML integration technology
    \item \textbf{Revenue Streams:} Multi-model (projects, subscriptions, apps, consulting)
    \item \textbf{Break-even Timeline:} Month 10, Year 1
    \item \textbf{Expected 3-Year ROI:} 187\% (85-95\% IRR)
\end{itemize}

\vspace{1cm}

\begin{importantbox}
    Bangladesh's GIS services market is growing quickly, driven by demand from agriculture, government, and real estate. Photogrammetry already has several local providers, but LiDAR combined with data science remains largely unexplored. That gap is the window --- and it won't stay open indefinitely.
\end{importantbox}
